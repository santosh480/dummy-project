Few facts about the world we live in

Earth

Earth is the third planet from the Sun and the only known planet that supports life. It is covered by vast oceans, mountains, forests, and deserts, creating a diverse environment for millions of species. The planet has a protective atmosphere that provides oxygen and helps regulate temperature. Earth rotates on its axis, causing day and night, and revolves around the Sun, resulting in different seasons. People depend on Earth's natural resources for food, water, energy, and shelter. Protecting the environment and using resources responsibly are important for maintaining a healthy planet for future generations.

  • Earth is the third planet from the Sun.

  • The planet's atmosphere provides oxygen and regulates temperature.
  • Earth experiences day and night due to its rotation.
  • Seasons change as Earth revolves around the Sun.
  • Natural resources are vital for human survival.
  • Environmental protection is crucial for future generations.
